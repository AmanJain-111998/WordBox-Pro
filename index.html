How To Play

Guess the word in 6 tries. Each guess must be a valid 5-letter word.

The color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.

Difficulty Levels

  • Easy: High-frequency words with common letters. Great for starting out.
  • Medium: Common words with moderate letter variations and occasional traps.
  • Hard: Uncommon words, double letters, or rare letters (Q, Z, X, J, K). A true challenge!

This app works 100% offline! Install it to your home screen to play anywhere.
